Getting Started

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ▼
Click the "Get Started" button on the homepage, enter your email and password, and verify your email address. You'll be able to start investing immediately after verification.
What is the minimum investment amount? ▼
You can start investing with as little as $1. Our automated investment plans allow you to set up recurring contributions at any amount that fits your budget.
Are there any fees? ▼
TeslasCap charges a 0.5% annual management fee on assets under management. There are no hidden fees, trading commissions, or account maintenance charges.
How secure is my account? ▼
We use enterprise-grade encryption, two-factor authentication, and comply with all financial regulations. Your assets are protected by SIPC insurance up to $500,000.
Investing

Investment Questions

What is an automated investment plan? ▼
An automated investment plan allows you to set up recurring deposits that are automatically invested according to your chosen strategy. You can set daily, weekly, or monthly contributions.
Can I withdraw my money anytime? ▼
Yes, you can withdraw your funds anytime without penalties. Withdrawals typically process within 1-3 business days depending on your bank.
What is tax-loss harvesting? ▼
Tax-loss harvesting automatically sells losing positions to offset gains and reduce your tax liability. This strategy can save you thousands in taxes each year.
How often is my portfolio rebalanced? ▼
Your portfolio is automatically rebalanced quarterly to maintain your target allocation. You can also manually rebalance anytime from your dashboard.
Trading

Stock & Crypto Trading

What are the trading hours? ▼
Stock trading is available during regular market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET). Crypto trading is available 24/7. Extended hours trading is available for premium members.
Is there a day trading limit? ▼
If your account is below $25,000, you're limited to 3 day trades per 5-day period. Accounts with $25,000+ have unlimited day trading.
How do I buy cryptocurrency? ▼
Fund your account via bank transfer or debit card, then use the Crypto Wallet section to buy from 50+ supported cryptocurrencies with instant execution.
What is the spread on crypto trades? ▼
Crypto spreads vary by asset and market conditions, typically ranging from 0.5% to 2%. Bitcoin and Ethereum have the tightest spreads.
