Compliance & Regulations

TeslasCap maintains the highest standards of regulatory compliance and financial industry best practices.

Regulatory Oversight

TeslasCap is a registered investment advisor and broker-dealer subject to comprehensive regulatory oversight. We comply with all applicable federal and state securities laws.

SEC Registration Registered
Registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as an investment advisor under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940.
FINRA Membership Member
Member of the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA), ensuring compliance with industry standards and investor protection rules.
SIPC Protection Protected
Securities Investor Protection Corporation (SIPC) protection covers cash and securities up to $500,000 per account.
State Licensing Licensed
Licensed in all states where required to conduct investment advisory and brokerage business.

Compliance Programs

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

We maintain comprehensive AML policies and procedures to detect and prevent money laundering and terrorist financing. All customers undergo Know Your Customer (KYC) verification.

Customer Identification Program (CIP)

We collect and verify customer identity information including name, address, date of birth, and government-issued ID to comply with CIP requirements.

Suspicious Activity Reporting (SAR)

We monitor transactions for suspicious activity and file Suspicious Activity Reports with FinCEN when required by law.

Best Execution

We are committed to obtaining the best execution for all customer orders across all asset classes, including stocks, options, and cryptocurrencies.

Suitability & Fiduciary Duty

Our advisors are held to a fiduciary standard, requiring us to act in your best interest and recommend only suitable investments.

Cybersecurity

We maintain robust cybersecurity measures including encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits to protect customer data.

Professional Certifications

Our team members hold industry-recognized certifications demonstrating expertise and commitment to professional standards:

  • CFP (Certified Financial Planner): Demonstrates expertise in financial planning and investment management
  • CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst): Advanced credential in investment analysis and portfolio management
  • Series 7: General Securities Representative License
  • Series 24: General Securities Principal License
  • Series 65: Uniform Investment Adviser Law Examination
  • Series 66: Uniform Combined State Law Examination
