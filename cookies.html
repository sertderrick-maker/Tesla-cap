Cookie Policy

Learn how TeslasCap uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you when you return to our platform. They allow us to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly. They enable you to log in, maintain your session, and access secure features. You cannot disable these cookies.

Performance Cookies

We use these cookies to analyze how you use our platform, including which features you access and how long you spend on each page. This helps us improve our services.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences, such as language settings, layout preferences, and watchlist selections, so you don't have to reset them each time you visit.

Marketing Cookies

With your consent, we use marketing cookies to show you relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Cookie Details

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
session_id Essential Maintains your login session Session
user_preferences Functional Stores your account preferences 1 year
analytics_token Performance Tracks platform usage analytics 2 years
marketing_id Marketing Tracks marketing campaign effectiveness 1 year

Third-Party Cookies

We work with third-party service providers who may set their own cookies on our platform for analytics, advertising, and functionality purposes. These include:

  • Google Analytics for usage analytics
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Intercom for customer support

Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our platform.

How to Manage Cookies

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for recognizing Do Not Track signals, so TeslasCap does not respond to these requests. However, you can use other tools to control cookies as described above.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or technology. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
