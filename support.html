Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email address, create a strong password, and verify your email. You'll then be able to complete your KYC verification to unlock full features.

What is KYC verification and why do I need it?

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is a regulatory requirement that helps us comply with financial laws and prevent fraud. We'll need to verify your identity using a government-issued ID and proof of address. This typically takes 24-48 hours.

How do I deposit funds into my wallet?

You can deposit funds through multiple methods: bank transfer, credit/debit card, or cryptocurrency. Go to your Wallet page, click "Deposit", select your preferred method, and follow the instructions. Deposits typically appear within 1-3 business days.

What are the fees for trading?

Our trading fees vary based on your VIP membership level. Standard users pay 2% per trade, while VIP members receive discounts up to 15% cashback. Check your VIP Membership page for detailed fee structures.

How do I withdraw my funds?

To withdraw funds, go to your Wallet page and click "Withdraw". Select your preferred withdrawal method (bank transfer or cryptocurrency), enter the amount, and confirm. Withdrawals are processed within 2-5 business days depending on the method.

Is my account secure?

Yes! We use industry-leading encryption, two-factor authentication, and cold storage for cryptocurrencies. We recommend enabling 2FA in your Account Settings for additional security. Your funds are protected by our insurance policy.
